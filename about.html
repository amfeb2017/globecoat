Globecoat India Private Limited

Globecoat India Private Limited, a subsidiary company of Globecoat Headquartered in Dubai, U.A.E. Globecoat has been active in wall and floor decorative finishes to international standards since 1995. GlobeCoat India is sole representative of Armourcoat Surface finishes in India.

Globecoat, UAE has a team of more than 200 trained applicators and has been an integral part of many prestigious Architectural, Residential and Commercial projects in the G.C.C. and around. In India we have appointed region wise authorized Dealers for the application process.

Armourcoat's Responsibilities Towards the Environment

One of Armourcoat's priorities is its commitment to a clean, green and healthy environment

Armourcoat's products are mineral based and procured in Europe. Armourcoat's products have low VOC(volatile organic compound) with 10% to 25% recycled content per Sq.ft. Armourcoat's supply Manufacturers have provided them with 'Lead Statement' and 'Green Matrix Statement'.
