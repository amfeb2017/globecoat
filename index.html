Globecoat

Globecoat has been active in wall and floor decorative finishes to international standards since 1995.

We are into diverse fields of activity and are dedicated to serving our customers and community as a whole. As a leading supplier and applicator of international quality polished innovative surface finishes and sculpture effects, We have enjoyed being an integral part of many prestigious, architectural, residential and commercial projects in the G.C.C. and around. Successfully we have also executed projects in Mauritius, Seychelles, Pakistan, Algeria, Nigeria, Ukraine, Maldives, Thailand, Eritrea, Sudan, Azerbaijan and Tanzania.
